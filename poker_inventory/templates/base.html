Poker Hand API Documentation

Quickstart Guide

https://poker1230.pythonanywhere.com/<hole1>/<hole2>&<flop1>/<flop2>/<flop3>/<turn>/<river>

Example: https://poker1230.pythonanywhere.com/5h/5c&10s/Jd/2c/5s/9d

If you visit the example link above the JSON object you see here will be returned. The key is your poker hand and the value is the 5 best cards that make up your hand. hole1 and hole2 are the user's cards and flop1, flop2, flop3, turn, and river are the community cards on the board
